Leave entitlements

Annual leave entitlements are automatically assigned based on role:


RoleAnnual entitlement
Supervisor (Spvr / M.Spvr)21 days
Maintenance Officer21 days
All other staff18 days

Leave counts only working days — weekends and public holidays are excluded.

Staff may carry over unused leave from previous years using the carry-over calculator on the Submit tab.

🎁 Gift leave — Maternity & Paternity

Maternity and paternity leave are gift leave — they are fully additional and do not reduce a staff member's annual leave entitlement.


Leave typeStandard durationDeducts from annual balance?
🤱 Maternity leave3 months (~65 working days)No — gift leave
👶 Paternity leave9 working daysNo — gift leave

After completing their maternity or paternity leave, staff may still apply for their full annual leave entitlement (18 or 21 days depending on role).

When submitting a maternity or paternity request, the system automatically fills in the standard duration and clearly marks it as gift leave so it is never counted against the annual balance.
